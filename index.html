how we work

Nestled in the heart of Kibera's Sarang'ombe Ward, NANE EXPERIENCE COMMUNITY BASED ORGANISATION stands as a beacon of hope and a catalyst for positive change. We are a grassroots organization deeply committed to uplifting the lives of vulnerable individuals and fostering a thriving community Our multifaceted approach addresses critical needs through six core programs.
