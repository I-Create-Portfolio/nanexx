PEOPLE IMPACTED

Girls and Women Empowerment

We believe in the power of women and girls to shape their own destinies. Our empowerment program focuses on providing essential knowledge and resources to navigate the challenges of adolescence and womanhood. We conduct vital workshops on menstrual hygiene, reproductive health, and the prevention of early pregnancies and marriages. We extend our reach beyond classrooms, visiting schools to distribute sanitary pads and conducting door-to-door outreach to support women and girls in need.

Dance Program

Dance is more than just an activity; it's a powerful tool for therapy and empowerment, especially for children growing up in challenging environments like the Kibera slums. By engaging kids in dance, we provide them with a positive outlet and a safe space, helping them build a brighter future away from the common struggles of crime and drug abuse.

Education

Education is the cornerstone of progress. Our scholarship program provides a lifeline to vulnerable children in Kibera, ensuring they have access to quality education. We cover school fees and provide all necessary learning materials, empowering these children to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future.

Library Program

Our library program aims to promote literacy and a love for reading among children and young adults. We provide access to a wide range of books and learning materials, creating a safe and inspiring space for students to explore their interests and develop their skills. Through reading clubs, storytelling sessions, and educational workshops, we empower the next generation with knowledge and creativity.

Youth Empowerment Program

Our youth empowerment program focuses on equipping young people with the skills and resources they need to succeed. We offer vocational training, mentorship, and access to job opportunities, helping them to build a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

Art Program

Our art program focuses on nurturing creativity and self-expression among young people. We provide access to various art forms, including visual arts, music, and performing arts, encouraging participants to explore their talents and develop their skills. Through workshops, exhibitions, and community projects, we empower the next generation to use art as a tool for personal and social change.

HOW WE WORK

Our holistic approach empowers individuals by addressing their essential physical, social, and economic needs, enabling them to lead their own sustainable change and thrive.
